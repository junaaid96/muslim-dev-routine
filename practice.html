Sunnah & health anchors

  • Plan and set intention after Fajr — early hours carry barakah.
  • Protect deep work between Fajr and Dhuhr; treat Salah as a reset, not an interruption.
  • Qaylulah: short midday nap (≈20 min) to restore afternoon focus.
  • Eat with Bismillah, in moderation — one-third food, drink, and air.
  • Movement is an amanah: walk, stretch, or strength work daily.
  • Maghrib closes the workday boundary; evening is for family and calm.
  • Before sleep: Al-Mulk, the three Quls, sleep on the right side, screens off.
  • Optional weekly: Surah Al-Kahf on Friday; fasting Monday & Thursday.
Private & local. Checklist, notes, water, and timer sessions are stored in this browser only (localStorage). They reset by calendar day for checklist/water; notes stay dated so you can revisit yesterday’s muhasabah.
